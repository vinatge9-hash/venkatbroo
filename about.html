Our Story

Founded by dog lovers, Happy Tails grew from a small neighborhood dog walking service into a full-service dog care provider. We focus on compassionate, individualized care and evidence-based training.

Mission
To keep dogs healthy, socialized, and joyful while supporting their families with education and reliability.
Values
Compassion, trust, safety, and continuous learning.

Our Approach

We combine professional training methods with enrichment activities and careful health monitoring. Every dog receives an intake consult to tailor care to their personality, age and health.

Safety First
All staff are trained in pet first aid; we maintain secure facilities and strict cleaning protocols.
Transparent Communication
Photos, updates and clear notes after every session so you never wonder how your dog spent their day.
Continuous Training
We share exercises to continue progress at home and strengthen the bond between you and your dog.
